Domestic Violence by County in New York.

Our Database pulls data from NY.Gov and gets its information from statistics about abuse victims in New York State. These victims are then seperated by county in which the crime was committed. From there we take the data and put it into our Database and that allows us to sort it in anyway that we want.

The chart above was created by pooling all the data that we got across various counties in New York. This shows the alarming difference between Domestic Violence crimes based on gender.
